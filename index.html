Transforming Outpatient Care in India's Tier 2 & 3 Cities

Ezy Op is a HealthTech platform focused on improving outpatient services by offering hospitals a free, user-friendly Hospital Management Software (HMS) and patients an easy way to book OP appointments online.

Reduced Waiting Times Enhanced Patient Experience Bridging Digital Infrastructure Gaps

Key Features

Comprehensive solutions for hospitals and patients

Free HMS for Hospitals

Provides hospitals with free, user-friendly Hospital Management Software (HMS) to digitize OPD systems, eliminating manual records and mismanagement.

User-Friendly Patient App

Allows patients to book OPD appointments anytime, from anywhere, eliminating long waits and unnecessary travel for confirmation.

Real-Time OPD Tracking System

Offers live updates on appointment status, improving patient communication and helping hospitals manage queues to prevent overcrowding.

Digital Medical Records

Stores consultations and prescriptions digitally, giving patients easy access to their medical history and providing hospitals with a structured patient database for better care.

Seamless Hospital Onboarding

Simple one-click onboarding process with no technical expertise required, offering 24/7 support to ensure smooth hospital transitions.

OP Tracking via WhatsApp

Send appointment reminders and live updates via WhatsApp, reducing no-shows and improving communication.

Hybrid OP Booking

Manage both online and walk-in patients with real-time token generation and doctor availability.

Smart Token-Based Appointment

Appointment booking only starts when the receptionist confirms doctor availability, reducing overcrowding.

15-Day Follow-Up & Free Rebook

Every booking includes a 15-day free follow-up, encouraging patient retention and rebooking.

Same-Day Rescheduling

Reschedule appointments based on real-time doctor availability, offering flexibility and convenience.
